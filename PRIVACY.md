# Privacy Policy — ContribTracker

**Last updated:** March 4, 2026

## Summary

ContribTracker is a desktop widget that displays your GitHub contribution data. **All data stays on your device.** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

## Data We Access

- **GitHub Username:** Used to fetch your public contribution data from GitHub's API.
- **GitHub Personal Access Token (optional):** Used to fetch more accurate contribution data. Stored locally on your device only.

## Data Storage

- Your username and optional token are stored in your device's local storage (Electron `localStorage`).
- Cached contribution data is stored locally and refreshed every 30 minutes.
- Window position and settings are stored in your `AppData` folder.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect analytics or telemetry.
- We do **not** send data to any external server (other than GitHub's official API).
- We do **not** use cookies or tracking pixels.
- We do **not** share any data with third parties.

## Third-Party Services

- **GitHub API:** We access GitHub's public REST API and GraphQL API to fetch your contribution data. GitHub's own [Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement) applies to their services.

## Your Rights

You can delete all stored data at any time by:

1. Clicking the "Change account" button in the widget.
2. Uninstalling the application.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/akshithnallaginnela/github-widget).
